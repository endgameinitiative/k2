# Goals 

The Goals for the endgame are to ensure Gitcoin is:

1. as **successful** as possible in achieving it's mission.
2. **sustainable** financially & socially
3. **decentralized** at the layers of governance, computation, tokenholder, and computation.
4. **reduce overhead** by cutting scope creep relentlessly focus.
5. **force trade-offs** in timing, resourcing, and other pragmatic ways to chart a course to the above goals.
