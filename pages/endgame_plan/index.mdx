# Endgame plan

This is an endgame plan conceived of & maintained by Kevin Owocki & Kyle Weiss in August 2023.  It is 

1. NOT official Gitcoin policy.
2. NOT financial, operational, taxation, or business advice.
2. heavily under construction.
3. a living & evolving document.
4. an invitation to the community to coordinate.

Read the endgame plan by navigating through the following resources

1. Early Game - covering Gitcoin's history 2017-2023.
2. Goals - a view on where we are now.
3. Mid Game - details concerning 2023 - 2025.
4. End Game - details concerning 2025+.
